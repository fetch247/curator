Chapter 2: Signature Recipes

Brownie Lovers

Taste the Italian tradition with a greedy twist. Espresso meets almond brownie syrup, topped with crumbled brownies and nuts. Warm, decadent, unapologetic.

Cherry Blossom Latte

Experience the sweetness of Sakura. Milk, cherry blossom syrup and espresso, finished with a flourish of strawberry and chocolate sauce.

Mistress Butterscotch

The flavor that brings back sweet memories. Butterscotch syrup, espresso, nut milk and caramel drizzle. Best enjoyed during golden hour.

Hazelnut Latte

Espresso layered over hazelnut syrup, topped with whipped cream, grated hazelnut and cocoa dust. Comfort in a cup.

Macadaccino

A macadamia latte with a creamy finish. Espresso, milk and macadamia syrup — a hug disguised as caffeine.

Pistachio Gingerbread Shake

Melted white chocolate meets pistachio ice cream and gingerbread syrup. Half milkshake, half dessert.

Cookie + Cream Affogato

Dark chocolate, caramel syrup and ice cream blended to velvet. Pour over espresso. Indulgence squared.
