Music Curation for Coffee Shops: Why Your Cafe Needs a Soundtrack, Not a Shuffle

Walk into any coffee shop that feels right and you will notice the music before you can name a single song. That is not an accident — it is curation. Decoded Music Playlist Curation builds sound programs for coffee shops the way a barista dials in espresso: by taste, by time of day, and by the room. A 7 a.m. opener is not an 11 a.m. rush track, and neither belongs at closing time. Our playlists move with your day — warm instrumental and lo-fi at open, clean upbeat energy for the rush, chill R&B and indie electronic through the afternoon lull, and a soft landing at last call.

Everything we program is workplace-clean and mood-mapped, anchored by the catalog of Rue de Vivre — also known as RDV, French-adjacent shorthand for the "Street of Life." Rue de Vivre's releases span lo-fi hip-hop, tropical house, soulful beats, and indie electronic — the exact palette coffee culture runs on. You can hear the catalog on Apple Music, Amazon Music, Spotify, and YouTube.
